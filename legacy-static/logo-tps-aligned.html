Design Rationale

Why it works
Scientific Flask × Pool Waves

Flask icon

The Erlenmeyer flask is the universal shorthand for science and expertise — directly reinforcing the "Scientist" in The Pool Scientist. Its open neck and expanding body visualize "sin fronteras" (no limits, no borders).

Pool waves inside

Water waves filling the flask's base ground the mark unmistakably in the pool industry, while the blue gradient echoes the TPS cerulean (#009EDB) — unifying both brands at first glance.

Atom orbit rings

Electron orbits encircling the flask reinforce the scientific/educational DNA and add a sense of energy and motion. They also subtly echo the "fronteras" idea: orbits have no borders — they loop forever.

Typography

Outfit 900 for "PISCINAS" — geometric and bold, matching The Pool Scientist's authority. Outfit 300 italic-weight spacing on "sin fronteras" adds contrast and elegance. "by THE POOL SCIENTIST" ties the sub-brand visually.
