02 — Clear Space & Minimum Size

Give the Logo Room

Always maintain a protected zone around the logo equal to the height of the "P" in PISCINAS (≈ 1× the cap-height unit). Never crowd the logo with text, images, or other graphic elements.

The dashed border represents the minimum clear space. No visual elements should enter this zone. The minimum recommended display size is 200px wide for digital and 40mm wide for print.

03 — Logo Don'ts

What to Avoid

To protect brand integrity, never alter the logo in the ways shown below.

Don't

Desaturate or convert to grayscale without an approved mono version

Don't

Rotate or tilt the logo

Don't

Stretch or distort proportions

Don't

Place on non-brand color backgrounds

Don't

Swap the Montserrat typeface for another font

Don't

Use the logo below the minimum size (200px / 40mm)

06 — Logo Anatomy

Elements of the Mark

The logo is composed of four distinct elements that work as a unified system.

The Erlenmeyer flask is the icon, always placed to the left of the wordmark. The atom orbit rings reinforce the scientific/educational identity. Pool waves fill the base of the flask, grounding it in the aquatics industry. The wordmark pairs Black-weight "PISCINAS" with Light-weight "sin fronteras" for elegant typographic contrast, followed by the sub-brand lockup "by THE POOL SCIENTIST."

07 — Sub-brand

Relationship with The Pool Scientist

Piscinas Sin Fronteras is a brand under The Pool Scientist (TPS). The sub-brand lockup "by THE POOL SCIENTIST" must always appear as part of the primary logo.

Sub-brand Lockup

The text "by THE POOL SCIENTIST" appears beneath the divider rule in Montserrat Regular 400, all caps, with extended letter-spacing. It is rendered in Pool Blue (#009EDB) on light backgrounds and in 45% white on dark backgrounds.

Always Visible

The sub-brand text must never be removed, hidden, or resized independently. It scales proportionally with the full logo. It should be legible at all intended display sizes.

Color Alignment

The TPS color palette is #009EDB (Pool Blue) and #00C3FF (Aqua Cyan). Piscinas Sin Fronteras uses the same palette, reinforcing the visual connection between parent and sub-brand.
